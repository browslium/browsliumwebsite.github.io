Privacy and data requests

Account deletion

To request deletion of your Browslium account, email us with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Organization-managed accounts may require administrator action.

support@browslium.com

Managed-device deployments

For schools, businesses, families, or organizations using Browslium on managed devices, include your organization name, device type, operating system version, and deployment method when requesting support.

Before contacting support

Information that helps us troubleshoot faster.

  • Browslium app version
  • Device model and operating-system version
  • Whether the device is personally owned or managed by an organization
  • Description of the filtering rule, website, app, or issue
  • Screenshot or error message, if available
